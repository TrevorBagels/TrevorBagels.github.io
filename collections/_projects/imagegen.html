IMAGE GENERATION

I wrote an image generation program, which took several equations, randomized them, and used them to generate color values based off of a pixel's position in the image. Over 2,000 images were generated by my program, which I left running every night for a couple of weeks, and the images here are all the ones that I found to be the best.
