Stages of hacking


The 5 stages of hacking
1) Reconnaissance - info gathering. passive (info gathering doesn't involve the client themselves)/active(things done involving the client (like scanning and stuff))
2) Scanning and enumeration (Nmap, Nessus, Nikto, metasploit, etc)
3) Gaining access
4) Maintaining access - don't get detected, don't get disconnected
5) Covering tracks
