I'm Trevor Bagels, a composer/programmer based in Portland, Oregon. I have several years of experience, and I spend most of my time making music and doing little programming side-projects. PROJECTS


Welcome, Primitive Lifeform, To Space

A tabletop RPG with an extensive amount of lore, as well as a WIP videogame, as well as a soundtrack with different themes for different space things.

Music Fingerprinting and Recognition

A program that utilizes machine learning to compare a given audio file to a database of songs, eventually returning the most similar song in our database. I developed the frontend and backend integration using NodeJS (and a bit of Python), while the other team members focused on the machine learning side of things using Python.

Bee-Estate (WIP)

A videogame where you break into houses at night, place beehives in those houses, buy the houses when people move out of them at a discounted price, remove the beehive, and then sell the house at its regular value. The gameplay revolves around stealth, and it heavily focuses on using lights and shadows to your advantage. The latest devlog can be viewed here.
