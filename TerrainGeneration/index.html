Temperature Fun

Usage

  1. Use WASD to move around
  2. Press "Proceed to next step"
  3. Press "Temperature Controls", then click on "Thermal View".
    You can press "Change scale of temperature" to change how things look.
  4. Press "Proceed to next step"
  5. Click on "Selected Cell Controls" and then click "Cell temperature control"
  6. You can enter an amount in the input field that was generated, and press + or - to modify the temperature of a cell. Try using big numbers, it looks cool.
