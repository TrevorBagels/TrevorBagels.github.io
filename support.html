Support Me

At the moment, I don't have a stable job (I'm a student), so any support is greatly appreciated! I try to do my part in giving back by making open source projects, music (which is free on YouTube if you don't have access to music streaming services), and answering questions that people have about various things.

How can you help?

  • Take a look at some of my work! It's nice to know that the things I'm making are having some kind of impact on people, so something as simple as listening to a peice I composed, or looking at one of my projects, supports me. You're supporting me right now, by viewing my website! Thanks!
  • Give me something to do! I should probably be focusing on finding a job, but something like a bug in my code or an idea for a new project generally keeps me productive, and being productive is good for my mental state.
  • Feedback! We all need feedback and constructive critisism to improve ourselves, so I value feedback very much. More info can be found in the contact section of my site.
  • Share my existence with people! It helps me network!
  • If you'd like to support me in a monetary way, I accept cryptocurrency (Doge and Bitcoin.)
